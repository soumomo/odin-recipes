Let's cook Chicken Biryani!

Chicken Biryani Recipe:

Prep Time: 30 minutes

Cook Time: 1 hour

Serves: 4-6

Ingredients:

For the Rice:

  • 2 cups (400g) of basmati rice
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) of oil
  • 1/2 cup (120g) of ghee
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup (120g) of yogurt
  • 1/2 cup (120g) of milk
  • 1/2 cup (120g) of water
  • 1/2 teaspoon (2g) of saffron strands
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) of warm milk
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) of warm water
  • 1/2 teaspoon (2g) of salt

For the Chicken Marinade:

  • 1 pound (500g) of chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1/2 cup (120g) of yogurt
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) of oil
  • 1/4 cup (60g) of ghee
  • 1 tablespoon (15g) of ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tablespoon (15g) of red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon (2g) of turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon (2g) of garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon (2g) of salt

For the Biryani Masala:

  • 1/2 cup (120g) of oil
  • 1/2 cup (120g) of ghee
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon (15g) of ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tablespoon (15g) of red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon (2g) of turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon (2g) of garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon (2g) of salt
  • 1/2 cup (120g) of chopped mint leaves
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
    • /
  • 1 tsp nutmeg powder(optional)
  • 1 tsp mace powder(optional)

For Layering:

  • 1/2 cup (120g) of fried onions
  • 1/2 cup (120g) of chopped mint leaves
  • 1/2 cut lemon

Instructions:

Step 1: Marinate the Chicken:

  1. Combine all the ingredients for the chicken marinade in a large bowl.
  2. Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours or overnight.

Step 2: Prepare the Rice:

  1. Wash the basmati rice and soak in water for 30 minutes.
  2. Drain the rice and set aside.
  3. In a large pot, bring water to a boil.
  4. Add the rice and cook until it is 70% done.

Step 3: Cook the Chicken:

  1. Heat oil and ghee in a large pot.
  2. Add the marinated chicken and cook until it is 70% done.

Step 4:Layer the Biriyani:

  1. Layer the rice over the chicken.
  2. Top with fried onions, mint leaves, and lemon slices.
  3. Cover and cook on low heat for 30 minutes.
  4. Dot with ghee or butter and sprinkle fried onions, cashews, and raisins (if using).
  5. Cover and cook for another 10 minutes.

Step 5: Serve:

  1. Fluff the biryani with a fork.
  2. Serve hot with raita or salad

Enjoy your delicious Chicken Biryani!
