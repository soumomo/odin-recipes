Let's cook Aloo Poha!

Aloo Poha Recipe:

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 15 minutes

Serves: 2-3

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups (200g) of poha (flattened rice)
  • 2 medium potatoes, boiled, peeled
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 1-2 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon (2g) of mustard seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon (2g) of cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon (2g) of turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon (2g) of red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon (2g) of sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon (2g) of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon (2g) of lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons (30ml) of oil
  • 2 tablespoons (30g) of peanuts
  • 2 tablespoons (30g) of chopped coriander leaves

Optional Add-ons:

  • 1/4 cup boiled green peas
  • Sev(crispy chickpea noodles) for garnishing

Instructions:

Step 1:Prep the Poha:

  1. Rinse the poha in a colander under running water until they soften. Drain and set aside.
  2. Sprinkle salt and sugar over the poha. Mix gently and set aside

Step 2:Cook the potatoes

  1. Peel the boiled potatoes and cut them into small cubes.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and add mustard seeds and cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
  3. Add peanuts and fry until they turn light brown.
  4. Add chopped green chilies and onions. Saute until onions turn translucent.
  5. Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well.
  6. Add the cubed potatoes and mix well.
  7. Cover and cook for 2-3 minutes on low flame.

Step 3:Add Poha

  1. Add the soaked poha to the pan.
  2. Mix gently until everything is well combined.
  3. Cover and cook for 2-3 minutes on low flame.
  4. Turn off the flame and add lemon juice and chopped coriander leaves.
  5. Mix well and serve hot.
