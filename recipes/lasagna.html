Let's cook Lasagna!

Prep Time: 30 minutes
Cook Time: 1 hour
Serves: 6-8

Ingredients:

For the Meat Sauce:

  • 1 pound (500g) lean ground beef
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can (400g) of crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (400g) of tomato sauce
  • 1 can (400g) of tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) of water
  • 2 tablespoons (30g) of white sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons (7g) of dried basil leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon (2g) of fennel seeds
  • 1 teaspoon (5g) of Italian seasoning
  • 1 tablespoon (15g) of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon (1g) of ground black pepper
  • 4 tablespoons (60ml) of chopped fresh parsley
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces (450g) of ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 teaspoon (4g) of salt
  • 3/4 pound (340g) of mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 3/4 cup (180g) of grated Parmesan cheese

For the Cheese Mixture:

  • 16 ounces (450g) of ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 teaspoon (4g) of salt
  • 3/4 pound (340g) of mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 3/4 cup (180g) of grated Parmesan cheese

Other Ingredients:

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces (450g) of ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 teaspoon (4g) of salt
  • 3/4 pound (340g) of mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 3/4 cup (180g) of grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions:

  1. Cook the Meat Sauce:
    • In a large skillet, cook the ground beef (or sausage) over medium heat until browned. Drain excess fat.
    • Add onion and garlic to the skillet and cook until onion is tender.
    • Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, basil, oregano, sugar, salt, and pepper. Simmer uncovered for 20-25 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  2. Prepare the Cheese Mixture:
    • In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese, 1 cup mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, egg, parsley, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  3. Cook the Noodles:
    • Boil lasagna noodles in salted water according to package instructions. Drain and lay flat on a clean towel to prevent sticking.
  4. Assemble the Lasagna:
    • Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
    • In a 9x13-inch baking dish, spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom.
    • Lay 3-4 lasagna noodles on top of the sauce, slightly overlapping.
    • Spread a layer of the cheese mixture, followed by a layer of meat sauce.
    • Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of meat sauce on top.
    • Sprinkle remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese on top.
  5. Bake the Lasagna:
    • Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes.
    • Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
  6. Rest and Serve:
    • Let the lasagna rest for 10-15 minutes before slicing and serving.
